Our Process

Our process follows a clear, structured approach to XML conversion and data processing, ensuring accuracy, consistency, and predictable outcomes across every project.

The 4-Step Journey

From raw data to refined intelligence.

1
Step 1

Requirement Analysis

We start by understanding your project objectives, source data, and expected output formats. This step helps define a clear scope and ensures the XML conversion or data processing workflow is aligned with your technical needs.

  • Initial discussion to understand project goals
  • Review and sampling of source materials
  • Collection of DTD or schema requirements
  • Timeline definition and delivery planning
  • Workflow configuration based on project scope
2
Step 2

Data Conversion

In this stage, our team converts source content into structured formats using a balanced approach that combines automated processing with skilled manual review to maintain accuracy and consistency.

  • Automated preparation and format normalization
  • Structured XML tagging and content mapping
  • Schema-aligned checks during conversion
  • Progress monitoring and status updates
  • Refinement based on review outcomes
3
Step 3

Quality Checks

Each project goes through structured quality checks to verify data accuracy, consistency, and alignment with defined specifications before final delivery.

  • Automated validation against DTD and XSD rules
  • Manual review and content verification
4
Step 4

Client Delivery

We deliver processed data in the agreed formats along with clear documentation to support smooth handover and integration into your systems.

  • Secure data transfer or integration support
  • Delivery summary and processing details
  • Quality review notes and documentation
  • Post-delivery clarification and support
  • Client feedback for continuous improvement

Our Quality Guarantees

We approach every project with clearly defined quality practices that help ensure reliability, consistency, and transparent collaboration throughout the engagement.

  • Consistent focus on high data accuracy across deliverables
  • Dedicated coordination support throughout the project
  • Secure handling of sensitive and confidential information
  • Scalable workflows for small and large project volumes
  • Clear communication during key project phases
  • Revision support based on agreed project scope
